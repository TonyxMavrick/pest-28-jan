Privacy Policy

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Service records are typically maintained for 7 years.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
