Our Story

Founded in 2011, PestGuard Pro has been dedicated to providing safe, effective, and environmentally responsible pest control solutions to homeowners and businesses throughout the region.

What started as a small family-owned operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in pest management, serving thousands of satisfied customers each year.

Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and eco-friendly practices has earned us numerous industry awards and certifications.
