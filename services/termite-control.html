Signs of Termite Infestation

  • Mud tubes on exterior walls or foundation
  • Discarded wings near windows or doors
  • Hollow-sounding wood
  • Visible damage to wood structures
  • Frass (termite droppings) that look like sawdust
  • Buckling paint or tiny holes in drywall
  • Sagging floors or doors

Treatment Methods

Liquid Barrier Treatment

Creates a protective barrier around your property that eliminates termites on contact and prevents new colonies from entering.

Baiting Systems

Strategic placement of monitoring stations that attract and eliminate entire termite colonies.

Direct Wood Treatment

Application of termiticides directly to infested wood and vulnerable areas.

Fumigation (when necessary)

For severe infestations, whole-structure fumigation provides complete elimination.
